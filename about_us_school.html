Barner Christian Academy of Davao City

Barner Christian Academy of Davao City's (BCA) primary goal is character development through the knowledge of Jesus Christ as revealed in the Bible. Stress is placed upon developing the God-given talents and abilities of the child through a program which is committed to maximizing the full potential of each precious child. BCA seeks to provide each child with a Christian education (Proverbs 3:5,6), development of his or her unique skills, and achievement of personal academic excellence.

Since the primary reason for the existence of the school is spiritual in nature, BCA lays a firm foundation for a child to accept Jesus Christ as his personal Lord and Savior. (Mark 10:14,15). The child will acquire basic spiritual truths, a spiritually receptive attitude, and an early motivation to make this important decision as he is prompted by the Holy Spirit.

BLC views each child as a special person, a special gift from God (Psalm 127:3). Since we believe in the child, we aim to bring out the fullest potential in him. We have a holistic and Biblical view of the child in developmental perspectives. We provide the conditions and atmosphere wherein the child will grow in wisdom and stature, and in favor wit both God and Man (Luke 2:52).

BLC provides the physical environment wherein the child can explore his body's physical abilities, the way his body fits into space, and the full kinesthetic potential of this body.

BLC provides the atmosphere wherein the child can:

  • Risk himself into varied learning experiences
  • Experience processes that build concepts and the interrelatedness of things
  • Feel truly successful and happy with himself in his conquest for knowledge, confident of God's love and acceptance.

BLC provides the conditions wherein the child can:

  • - Fully relate to other children
  • - Intensify his confidence that the world is God's creation in which he can fully function and have positive experiences.

BLC provides an integrated curriculum which fosters active interaction with the environment through varied and rich experiences and materials.

OBJECTIVES

The BCA (Barner Christian Academy of Davao City) is a structured learning environment which aims to provide experiences in the total development of the child in order to:

  • Lay a firm foundation for a child to accept Jesus Christ as his personal Lord and Savior.
  • Inculcate in the child basic Christian values.
  • Develop self understanding and Biblical self-concept.
  • Stimulate the child to explore, understand and appreciate his environment through independence, training and multi-sensorial education.
  • Help the child achieve and maintain physical health through fine and complex motor activities.
  • Develop skills in listening, speaking, beginner reading, writing and mathematics, critical thinking and basic Bible concepts.
  • Encourage communicative skills, self expression and creativity through art, music and movement.
  • Assist the child in socially acceptable behavior for social participation in the home, school, church and community.
